fact about gt3rs

Fact: The center radiator concept was inspired from the 991 RSR. By using one central radiator instead of three, engineers were able to create a “S” duct that sent the air under the bumper and out through the hood. This also increased front downforce while reducing the overall weight by 15 pounds as compared to the three-radiator design.
